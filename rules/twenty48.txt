2048

Slide and merge tiles to reach the 2048 tile.

HOW TO PLAY
- Use the arrow keys to slide all tiles in a direction.
- Tiles with the same number merge into one when they collide.
- Each tile can only merge once per move.
- After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot.

SCORING
- Your score increases by the value of each merged tile.

WINNING AND LOSING
- Win: create a 2048 tile (you can keep playing after).
- Lose: no valid moves remain (board full, no adjacent matches).
