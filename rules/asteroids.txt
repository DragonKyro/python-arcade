ASTEROIDS

Destroy asteroids and survive as long as possible.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow: rotate your ship.
- Up arrow: thrust forward.
- Space: fire bullets.

GAMEPLAY
- Shoot asteroids to break them into smaller pieces.
- Large asteroids split into medium, medium into small.
- Small asteroids are destroyed completely.
- Your ship wraps around screen edges.
- Avoid colliding with asteroids — costs a life.

SCORING
- Large asteroid: 20 points.
- Medium asteroid: 50 points.
- Small asteroid: 100 points.

You start with 3 lives. A new wave spawns when all asteroids are cleared.
