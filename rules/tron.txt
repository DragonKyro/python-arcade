TRON (LIGHT CYCLES)

Leave a trail and survive longer than your opponents.

CONTROLS
- Arrow keys to change direction (Up/Down/Left/Right).
- You cannot reverse direction.

GAMEPLAY
- Your light cycle leaves a permanent trail behind it.
- Hitting any trail (yours or an opponent's) or a wall
  eliminates you.
- Choose the number of computer opponents (1-3) before starting.
- Last player alive wins the round.

STRATEGY
- Cut off opponents by boxing them in.
- Keep open space for yourself.
- Watch all trails, not just your own.
