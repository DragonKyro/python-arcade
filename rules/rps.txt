ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

Beat the computer in a best-of-many showdown.

HOW TO PLAY
- Click Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your choice.
- The computer makes its choice at the same time.

RULES
- Rock beats Scissors.
- Scissors beats Paper.
- Paper beats Rock.
- Same choice is a draw.

The computer studies your patterns, so try to stay unpredictable!
