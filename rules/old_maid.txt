OLD MAID

Avoid being the last player holding the unpaired Queen.

HOW TO PLAY

Players: 2-4.

Remove one Queen from the deck before dealing. Deal all remaining cards evenly.

Each player removes and discards any pairs from their hand.

On your turn, draw one card (without looking) from the hand of the player to your left. If it forms a pair, discard the pair.

Play continues until all pairs have been discarded. The player left holding the lone Queen (the Old Maid) loses.
