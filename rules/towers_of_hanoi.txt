TOWERS OF HANOI
Move an entire stack of disks between three pegs, never placing a larger disk on a smaller one.

HOW TO PLAY
- Three pegs hold a stack of disks sorted by size on the first peg.
- Move one disk at a time from the top of any peg to another peg.
- Rebuild the full stack on the last peg.

RULES
1. Only one disk may be moved at a time.
2. Only the top disk of a peg can be moved.
3. A larger disk may never be placed on top of a smaller disk.
4. The minimum number of moves for N disks is 2^N - 1.
5. All disks must end on the target peg to win.
