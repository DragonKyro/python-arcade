FLAPPY BIRD

Tap to fly through as many pipe gaps as possible.

CONTROLS
- Press Space or click the mouse to flap.
- Each flap gives a small upward boost.
- Gravity pulls the bird down constantly.

GAMEPLAY
- Pipes scroll from right to left with gaps to fly through.
- Each pipe pair you pass scores 1 point.
- The gap size and speed stay consistent — it's all about timing.

GAME OVER
- Hitting a pipe or the ground ends the game.
- Try to beat your high score!
