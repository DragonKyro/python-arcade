DOTS AND BOXES

Complete more boxes than the computer on a 5x5 grid.

HOW TO PLAY
- Click between two adjacent dots to draw a line.
- If your line completes a box (all 4 sides drawn), you score a
  point and get another turn.
- The computer follows the same rules.

STRATEGY
- Try to avoid drawing the third side of a box, as the opponent
  will complete it and score.
- Force the opponent into giving you chains of boxes.

WINNING
- The player with the most completed boxes when all lines are drawn wins.
