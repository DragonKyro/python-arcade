MINESWEEPER

Reveal all safe cells without detonating a mine.

HOW TO PLAY
- Left-click a cell to reveal it.
- Right-click a cell to place or remove a flag.
- Numbers show how many of the 8 neighboring cells contain mines.
- Empty cells (no adjacent mines) auto-reveal their neighbors.
- Your first click is always safe.

WINNING AND LOSING
- Win: all non-mine cells are revealed.
- Lose: you click on a mine.

The counter at the top shows remaining mines (total mines minus flags placed).
