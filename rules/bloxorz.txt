BLOXORZ
Roll a 1x1x2 block across a grid to land it upright on the goal hole.

HOW TO PLAY
- A rectangular block stands on a grid of tiles.
- Tilt the block in any cardinal direction to roll it.
- The block must end standing upright on the single goal square.

RULES
1. The block rolls by tipping over one edge in the chosen direction.
2. Every part of the block must remain on solid tiles; falling off the edge loses.
3. The goal is a single square hole; the block must be upright to fit into it.
4. Some tiles are switches that activate bridges or toggle platforms.
5. Fragile tiles break if the block stands upright on them.
