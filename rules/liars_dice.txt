LIAR'S DICE

Bluff and call bluffs in this dice-betting game.

SETUP
- Each player starts with 5 dice, rolled secretly.
- You can see your own dice but not others'.
- Choose the number of computer opponents (1-4) before starting.

HOW TO PLAY
- Players take turns making bids: "there are at least N dice
  showing face X among ALL players' dice."
- Each bid must be higher than the last (higher quantity, or same
  quantity with a higher face value).
- Instead of bidding, you can call "Liar!" on the previous bid.

CALLING LIAR
- All dice are revealed and counted.
- If the bid was correct (or exceeded), the caller loses a die.
- If the bid was wrong, the bidder loses a die.

WINNING
- A player with zero dice is eliminated.
- Last player standing wins.
