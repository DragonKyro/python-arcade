NURIKABE
Shade sea cells so each numbered cell anchors an island of that size, with a connected sea and no 2x2 black squares.

HOW TO PLAY
- A grid contains some numbered cells; the rest are blank.
- Shade blank cells to form the sea; unshaded cells form islands.
- Each number defines the size of its island.

RULES
1. Each number belongs to exactly one island of that many unshaded cells.
2. Each island contains exactly one numbered cell.
3. No 2x2 area may be entirely shaded (no pools in the sea).
4. All shaded (sea) cells must be orthogonally connected as one group.
5. Islands may not touch each other orthogonally.
