OTHELLO (REVERSI)

Capture the most pieces by flipping your opponent's discs.

HOW TO PLAY
- You are Black, the computer is White. Black goes first.
- Click a valid cell (shown with a dot) to place your disc.
- Your disc must bracket one or more opponent discs in a straight
  line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
- All bracketed opponent discs are flipped to your color.

SPECIAL RULES
- If you have no valid moves, your turn is skipped.
- The game ends when neither player can move.

WINNING
- The player with the most discs on the board wins.
