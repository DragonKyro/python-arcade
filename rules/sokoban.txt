SOKOBAN
Push boxes onto goal squares; you cannot pull them.

HOW TO PLAY
- You control a character in a grid-based warehouse.
- Walk into a box to push it one space in that direction.
- Push every box onto a marked goal square to complete the level.

RULES
1. You can only push one box at a time, never pull.
2. You cannot push a box into a wall or into another box.
3. A box pushed into a corner may become permanently stuck.
4. All goal squares must be covered by boxes to win.
5. Plan ahead; there is no undo in classic Sokoban.
