SPADES

Win the number of tricks you bid with your partner.

HOW TO PLAY

Players: 4 (2 teams of 2, partners sit across from each other).

Deal all 52 cards evenly (13 each). Spades are always trump.

Bidding: each player bids the number of tricks they expect to win. Team bids are combined.

The player to the dealer's left leads the first trick. Players must follow suit if possible. If unable, they may play any card (including a spade to trump). Highest card of the led suit wins unless trumped; highest spade wins if any were played.

Scoring:
- Meet your bid: score bid x 10 points.
- Fail your bid: lose bid x 10 points.
- Overtricks (bags): 1 point each. Every 10 accumulated bags = -100 point penalty.

First team to reach 500 points wins.
