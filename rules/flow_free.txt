FLOW FREE
Connect matching colored dots with pipes and fill the entire board.

HOW TO PLAY
- A grid contains pairs of colored dots.
- Draw a path (pipe) connecting each pair of same-colored dots.
- Pipes must fill every cell on the board.

RULES
1. Each pipe connects exactly two dots of the same color.
2. Pipes travel horizontally or vertically, never diagonally.
3. Pipes may not cross, overlap, or share cells.
4. Every cell on the board must be occupied by a pipe.
5. There is exactly one valid solution for each puzzle.
