BATTLESHIP

Sink all enemy ships before yours are sunk.

PLACEMENT PHASE
- Place your 5 ships on the left grid.
- Click to place a ship. Press R to rotate (horizontal/vertical).
- Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2).

BATTLE PHASE
- Click a cell on the right grid (enemy waters) to fire a shot.
- Red = hit, white dot = miss.
- You and the computer take turns firing.

WINNING
- Sink all 5 enemy ships to win. The game ends if all your ships are sunk.
