GOMOKU

A classic strategy board game also known as "Five in a Row."

HOW TO PLAY
- The game is played on a 15x15 grid.
- Black goes first, then players alternate turns.
- Click an empty intersection to place your stone.
- The first player to get exactly 5 stones in a row wins.
- Rows can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

RULES
- Players take turns placing one stone per turn.
- Stones cannot be moved or removed once placed.
- The game ends when a player forms an unbroken line of 5 stones.
- If the board fills up with no winner, the game is a draw.
