YAHTZEE

A dice game of risk and reward for 1-4 players across 13 rounds.

HOW TO PLAY
- Roll 5 dice at the start of each turn.
- Click dice to keep them, then reroll the rest (up to 2 additional rolls per turn).
- After rolling, choose a scoring category to fill for that round.
- After 13 rounds, the player with the highest total score wins.

RULES
- Upper Section: Ones through Sixes - score the sum of matching dice. Bonus 35 points if upper total is 63 or more.
- Three of a Kind: 3 matching dice, score sum of all dice.
- Four of a Kind: 4 matching dice, score sum of all dice.
- Full House: 3 of one kind + 2 of another, scores 25 points.
- Small Straight: 4 sequential dice, scores 30 points.
- Large Straight: 5 sequential dice, scores 40 points.
- Yahtzee: All 5 dice the same, scores 50 points. Bonus 100 for each additional Yahtzee.
- Chance: Score the sum of all dice (no requirements).
- Each category can only be used once. You may score 0 in a category if needed.
