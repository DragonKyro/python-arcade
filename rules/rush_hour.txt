RUSH HOUR
Slide cars on a grid to free the red car and reach the exit.

HOW TO PLAY
- A 6x6 grid contains vehicles of various sizes placed horizontally or vertically.
- Slide vehicles along their orientation to clear a path.
- Move the red car to the right edge exit.

RULES
1. Vehicles can only slide forward or backward along their orientation.
2. Vehicles cannot pass through or overlap other vehicles.
3. Only the red car needs to reach the exit on the right side.
4. Cars occupy 2 spaces; trucks occupy 3 spaces.
5. There is no limit on the number of moves, but fewer is better.
