BACKGAMMON

Bear off all 15 of your checkers before the computer.

HOW TO PLAY
- Click "Roll Dice" to roll two dice.
- Click a checker to select it, then click a valid destination
  (highlighted in green) to move it.
- You must use both dice if possible (or all 4 on doubles).

MOVEMENT
- You are White, moving from point 24 toward point 1.
- You can land on empty points, your own points, or points with
  exactly 1 opponent checker (a "blot" — it gets sent to the bar).

BAR AND BEARING OFF
- Checkers on the bar must re-enter before any other move.
- Once all your checkers are in your home board (points 1-6),
  you can bear them off. Exact or higher dice values work.

WINNING
- First player to bear off all 15 checkers wins.
