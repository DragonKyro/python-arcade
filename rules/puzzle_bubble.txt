PUZZLE BUBBLE (BUST-A-MOVE)

Shoot colored bubbles to match 3 or more and clear the board.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow: aim the shooter.
- Space: fire a bubble.

GAMEPLAY
- A bubble launches from the bottom center in the aimed direction.
- The bubble sticks where it hits other bubbles or the top wall.
- When 3 or more bubbles of the same color connect, they pop.
- Any bubbles hanging without a path to the top also fall.

SCORING
- Points for each bubble popped.
- Bonus for chain reactions (falling disconnected clusters).

GAME OVER
- The game ends if bubbles reach the bottom of the screen.
- Clear all bubbles to win the level.
