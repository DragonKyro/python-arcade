GO FISH

Collect the most sets of four matching cards.

HOW TO PLAY

Players: 2-4.

Each player is dealt 5 cards (7 if only 2 players). Remaining cards form the stock.

On your turn:
1. Ask any opponent for a specific rank you already hold.
2. If they have any cards of that rank, they give you all of them. You go again.
3. If they do not, they say "Go Fish." Draw one card from the stock.

When you collect all 4 cards of the same rank, place the set face-up in front of you.

The game ends when all sets have been collected. The player with the most sets wins.
