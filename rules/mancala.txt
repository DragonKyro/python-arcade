MANCALA (KALAH)

Collect the most stones in your store.

SETUP
- 6 pits per side, each starting with 4 stones.
- Your store is on the right; the computer's store is on the left.

HOW TO PLAY
- Click one of your pits (bottom row) to pick up its stones.
- Stones are sown one per pit counter-clockwise, including your
  store but skipping the opponent's store.

SPECIAL RULES
- Extra turn: if your last stone lands in your store, you go again.
- Capture: if your last stone lands in an empty pit on your side,
  you capture that stone plus all stones in the opposite pit.

WINNING
- The game ends when one side's pits are all empty.
- Remaining stones go to the other player's store.
- Highest store total wins.
