TETRIS VS

Compete head-to-head against the computer in split-screen Tetris.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow: move piece sideways.
- Down arrow: soft drop.
- Up arrow: rotate clockwise.
- Space: hard drop.

VS MECHANICS
- Clear 2+ lines at once to send garbage lines to your opponent.
- 2 lines = 1 garbage, 3 lines = 2 garbage, 4 lines (Tetris) = 4 garbage.
- Garbage lines appear at the bottom with one random gap.
- Your opponent plays by the same rules.

DIFFICULTY
- Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard before the match.
- Harder AI plays faster and makes smarter placement decisions.

WINNING
- Your opponent tops out (pieces stack past the top) = you win.
- You top out = you lose.
