15 PUZZLE

Slide tiles to arrange them in order from 1 to 15.

HOW TO PLAY
- Click a tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it.
- Or use arrow keys to move tiles into the empty space.
- Arrange all tiles in numerical order, left to right,
  top to bottom, with the empty space in the bottom-right.

WINNING
- The puzzle is solved when tiles read 1-15 in order.
- A move counter tracks how many slides you've made.
- Try to solve it in as few moves as possible!
