BLACKJACK

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

HOW TO PLAY

Players: 1 human + 1-3 AI players (optional) vs. dealer.
Bet before each hand.

Card Values:
- Number cards: face value
- Face cards (J, Q, K): 10
- Ace: 1 or 11

Actions:
- Hit: draw another card
- Stand: keep your current hand
- Double: double your bet, receive exactly one more card
- Split: if dealt a pair, split into two separate hands

Dealer hits on 16 or less, stands on 17 or more.
Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 3:2.
Going over 21 is a bust (automatic loss).
