HITORI
Shade cells to eliminate duplicate numbers in each row and column.

HOW TO PLAY
- A grid is filled with numbers, some of which repeat in rows or columns.
- Shade cells to remove duplicates so each row and column has unique unshaded numbers.
- Follow connectivity and adjacency constraints.

RULES
1. No unshaded number may appear more than once in any row or column.
2. Shaded cells may not be orthogonally adjacent to each other.
3. All unshaded cells must form a single connected group (orthogonally).
4. A shaded cell's value is considered removed from its row and column.
5. Use logic to determine which duplicates to shade.
