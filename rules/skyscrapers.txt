SKYSCRAPERS
Fill a grid with building heights so edge clues match the number of visible buildings from that direction.

HOW TO PLAY
- An NxN grid must be filled with heights 1 through N.
- Clues around the border indicate how many buildings are visible looking into that row or column.
- Taller buildings block shorter ones behind them.

RULES
1. Each row must contain heights 1 to N exactly once.
2. Each column must contain heights 1 to N exactly once.
3. A clue number shows how many buildings are visible from that edge.
4. A building is visible if no taller building stands between it and the observer.
5. A clue of 1 means the tallest building (N) is at the edge; a clue of N means heights are ascending.
