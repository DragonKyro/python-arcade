PICROSS
Fill grid cells using number clues along rows and columns to reveal a hidden image (also known as Nonograms).

HOW TO PLAY
- A grid has number clues listed for each row and column.
- Fill in cells to match the clue sequences.
- The completed grid reveals a pixel-art picture.

RULES
1. Each clue number represents a consecutive block of filled cells in that row or column.
2. Blocks appear in the order listed, from left-to-right or top-to-bottom.
3. At least one empty cell must separate consecutive blocks.
4. Cells that are not part of any block remain empty.
5. Every puzzle has a unique solution solvable by logic alone.
