RICOCHET ROBOTS
Slide robots that move until hitting a wall or another robot to reach a target.

HOW TO PLAY
- A grid contains several robots and a designated target square.
- Move any robot in a cardinal direction; it slides until blocked.
- Get the target robot onto the target square.

RULES
1. A robot slides in a straight line until it hits a wall or another robot.
2. You may move any robot, not just the target robot, to create blockers.
3. The goal is to reach the target in the fewest total moves.
4. Robots cannot move diagonally.
5. All robots remain where they stop after each move.
