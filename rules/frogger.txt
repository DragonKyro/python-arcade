FROGGER

Guide your frog across busy roads and a river to reach home.

CONTROLS
- Arrow keys to hop: Up, Down, Left, Right.
- Each press moves the frog one step.

ROAD (bottom half)
- Dodge cars and trucks moving across the lanes.
- Getting hit costs a life.

RIVER (top half)
- Jump onto logs and turtles to cross the water.
- Falling in the water costs a life.
- Ride logs and turtles as they move sideways.

WINNING
- Reach one of the home spots at the top.
- Fill all home spots to complete the level.

You start with 3 lives. There is a time limit for each frog.
