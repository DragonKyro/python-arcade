GALAGA

Destroy waves of alien fighters in this classic space shooter.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow keys to move your ship.
- Space to fire.

ENEMIES
- Bees (blue): basic fighters, simple dive attacks.
- Butterflies (red): aggressive, wide swooping attack patterns.
- Boss Galaga (green): takes 2 hits, may use tractor beam to
  capture your ship. Destroy a boss holding your ship to get
  a dual-fighter (double firepower!).

GAMEPLAY
- Enemies form in waves at the top, then dive-bomb your ship.
- Destroy all enemies to advance to the next stage.
- Bonus stages appear periodically (no enemies fire back).
- You start with 3 lives.

SCORING
- Bees: 50 points (formation) / 100 (diving).
- Butterflies: 80 (formation) / 160 (diving).
- Boss: 150 (formation) / 400 (diving).
