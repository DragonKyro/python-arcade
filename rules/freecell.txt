FREECELL

Single-deck solitaire with all 52 cards dealt face up.

HOW TO PLAY

- All 52 cards are dealt face up into 8 tableau columns (4 columns of 7, 4 columns of 6).
- 4 free cells (top left) provide temporary storage for single cards.
- 4 foundation piles (top right) are built up by suit from Ace to King.
- Build tableau columns in descending order, alternating red and black suits.
- Only the top card of each column may be moved.
- Move a single card to a free cell, an empty column, or a valid tableau position.
- Multiple cards may be moved as a shortcut if enough free cells and empty columns exist.
- The number of cards you can move at once equals (1 + free cells open) x 2^(empty columns).
- An empty tableau column may be filled with any card.
- Move Aces to foundations as they become available, then build up by suit.
- Win by moving all 52 cards to the 4 foundation piles.
