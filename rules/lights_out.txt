LIGHTS OUT
Toggle a light and its neighbors to turn all lights off.

HOW TO PLAY
- You are presented with a grid of lights, some on and some off.
- Click any light to toggle it and its orthogonal neighbors (up, down, left, right).
- Your goal is to turn all lights off.

RULES
1. Toggling a light switches it and all directly adjacent lights.
2. Lights on the edges or corners have fewer neighbors.
3. Every puzzle has a solution, but order of moves matters for efficiency.
4. The fewest moves possible is the optimal solution.
5. There is no undo unless the game provides one; plan carefully.
