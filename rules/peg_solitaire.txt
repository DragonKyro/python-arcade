PEG SOLITAIRE

Remove pegs by jumping until only one remains.

HOW TO PLAY
- The board starts full of pegs with one empty hole in the center.
- Click a peg to select it, then click an empty hole to jump.
- A peg can jump over an adjacent peg into an empty hole beyond it.
- The jumped-over peg is removed from the board.
- Jumps can be horizontal or vertical (not diagonal).

WINNING
- Win by leaving exactly one peg on the board.
- The game ends when no more jumps are possible.

STRATEGY
- Work from the edges toward the center.
- Avoid isolating single pegs with no neighbors to jump.
