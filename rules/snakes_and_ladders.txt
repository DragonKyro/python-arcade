SNAKES AND LADDERS

A classic race-to-the-finish board game of luck for 2-6 players.

HOW TO PLAY
- Choose the number of AI opponents (1-5) before starting.
- Players take turns rolling a single die and moving forward that many spaces.
- Navigate the numbered board from square 1 to square 100.
- The first player to land on or reach square 100 wins.

RULES
- Landing on the bottom of a ladder moves you up to the top of that ladder.
- Landing on the head of a snake slides you down to its tail.
- You must land exactly on 100 to win; excess rolls do not count or bounce back.
- Players move in turn order. Roll the die by clicking the roll button.
- The game continues until one player reaches square 100.
