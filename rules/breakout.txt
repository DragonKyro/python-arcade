BREAKOUT

Destroy all bricks by bouncing a ball off your paddle.

CONTROLS
- Move your paddle with the mouse (or Left/Right arrow keys).
- Click or press Space to launch the ball.

GAMEPLAY
- The ball bounces off walls, the paddle, and bricks.
- Each brick hit destroys the brick and scores points.
- Different colored bricks are worth different points.
- The ball speeds up slightly as bricks are cleared.
- If the ball falls below your paddle, you lose a life.

SCORING
- Red bricks: 10 points.
- Orange bricks: 20 points.
- Green bricks: 30 points.
- Yellow bricks: 50 points.

You start with 3 lives. Clear all bricks to advance to the next level.
