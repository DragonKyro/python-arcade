HASHI
Connect numbered islands with one or two bridges; no crossing; all islands must form a single network.

HOW TO PLAY
- Circles with numbers (islands) are placed on a grid.
- Draw horizontal or vertical bridges between islands.
- Connect all islands into one connected group.

RULES
1. Bridges run in straight horizontal or vertical lines between two islands.
2. Each pair of islands may be connected by at most two bridges.
3. Bridges may not cross other bridges or pass through islands.
4. The number on an island equals the total bridges connected to it.
5. All islands must be reachable from any other island through bridges.
