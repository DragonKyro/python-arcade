PONG

Classic paddle game — score 10 points to win.

HOW TO PLAY
- Move your paddle with W (up) and S (down), or the Up/Down arrow keys.
- The ball bounces off the top and bottom walls and off paddles.
- Score a point when the ball passes your opponent's paddle.

DIFFICULTY
- Click Easy, Medium, or Hard before the game starts.
- Easy: AI reacts slowly and makes mistakes.
- Medium: AI tracks the ball with moderate speed.
- Hard: AI is fast and precise, but not perfect.

First to 10 points wins!
