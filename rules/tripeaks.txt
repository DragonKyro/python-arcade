TRI PEAKS SOLITAIRE

Clear three overlapping peaks by removing cards in sequence.

HOW TO PLAY

- 28 cards are dealt face up and face down forming 3 peaks of overlapping cards.
- Each peak has 4 rows; only fully uncovered cards are face up and available.
- Remaining 24 cards form the stock pile.
- One card is flipped from the stock to start the waste pile.
- Remove any available card that is exactly 1 rank higher or lower than the top waste card.
- Rank wraps around: a King may be played on an Ace, and an Ace on a King.
- The removed card becomes the new top of the waste pile, continuing the chain.
- When no available card matches, flip the next stock card onto the waste pile.
- A card becomes available (flipped face up) when both cards overlapping it are removed.
- Longer chains of consecutive removals score bonus points.
- Win by clearing all three peaks from the table.
