KLONDIKE SOLITAIRE

Classic single-deck solitaire card game.

HOW TO PLAY

- 7 tableau columns are dealt: column 1 gets 1 card, column 2 gets 2, up to column 7 with 7 cards.
- Only the top card of each column is face up; the rest are face down.
- Remaining cards form the stock pile.
- Flip cards from the stock to the waste pile.
- Build tableau columns in descending order, alternating red and black suits.
- Move any face-up card (and all cards on top of it) to another valid tableau column.
- When a face-down card is exposed, flip it face up.
- An empty tableau column may only be filled with a King (or a stack starting with a King).
- Move Aces to the 4 foundation piles, then build each foundation up by suit from Ace to King.
- Win by moving all 52 cards to the foundations.
