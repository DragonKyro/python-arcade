WAR

Win all the cards.

HOW TO PLAY

Players: 2.

Split the deck evenly between both players. Cards remain face down in a pile.

Each round, both players flip their top card. The higher card wins both cards (placed at the bottom of the winner's pile).

Tie = War:
1. Each player places 3 cards face down.
2. Each player flips 1 card face up.
3. Higher face-up card wins all cards in play.
4. If still tied, repeat the war.

The game ends when one player has all the cards.
