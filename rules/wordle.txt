WORDLE

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries.

HOW TO PLAY
- Type a 5-letter word using your keyboard and press Enter.
- Press Backspace to delete a letter.

FEEDBACK (color of each letter after guessing)
- Green: correct letter in the correct position.
- Yellow: correct letter but in the wrong position.
- Gray: letter is not in the word at all.

Use the feedback to narrow down the answer. The on-screen keyboard
also updates to show which letters have been used and their status.

You have 6 attempts. Good luck!
