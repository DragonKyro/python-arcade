SPACE INVADERS

Defend Earth by destroying waves of alien invaders.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow: move your ship.
- Space: fire a laser.

GAMEPLAY
- Aliens march sideways and drop down when they reach the edge.
- Destroy all aliens to advance to the next wave.
- Aliens speed up as fewer remain.
- Aliens occasionally fire back — dodge their shots!
- Shields provide temporary cover but degrade when hit.

SCORING
- Different alien rows are worth different points.
- Bonus UFO occasionally flies across the top for extra points.

GAME OVER
- You lose if an alien reaches your ship's row or you lose all lives.
- You start with 3 lives.
