SPIDER SOLITAIRE

Two-deck solitaire played on 10 tableau columns.

HOW TO PLAY

- 54 cards are dealt across 10 columns: columns 1-4 get 6 cards, columns 5-10 get 5 cards.
- Only the top card of each column is face up; the rest are face down.
- Remaining 50 cards form the stock.
- Build tableau columns in descending order regardless of suit.
- A sequence of cards in descending order of the same suit may be moved together as a group.
- When a face-down card is exposed, flip it face up.
- Any card or valid group may fill an empty column.
- A complete same-suit run from King down to Ace is automatically removed to a foundation pile.
- When no more moves are available, deal 10 new cards from the stock (one to each column).
- All columns must be non-empty before dealing from the stock.
- Win by completing all 8 same-suit King-to-Ace runs.
