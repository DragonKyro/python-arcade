MAHJONG SOLITAIRE
Remove matching pairs of free tiles from a layered layout.

HOW TO PLAY
- Tiles are stacked in a layered formation.
- Select two matching free tiles to remove them from the board.
- Clear all tiles to win.

RULES
1. A tile is free if no tile is on top of it and at least one side (left or right) is open.
2. Only free tiles may be selected.
3. Two tiles match if they show the same face (identical symbol).
4. Tiles are removed in pairs only.
5. Not all layouts are solvable; if no more matches exist among free tiles, the game is stuck.
