PYRAMID SOLITAIRE

Remove pairs of cards that sum to 13 to clear the pyramid.

HOW TO PLAY

- 28 cards are dealt face up in a pyramid of 7 rows (1 card in row 1, up to 7 in row 7).
- Each card partially overlaps two cards in the row below it.
- A card is available for play only when no cards overlap it from below.
- Remaining 24 cards form the stock pile.
- Remove pairs of available cards whose ranks sum to 13.
- Card values: A=1, 2-10=face value, J=11, Q=12, K=13.
- Kings equal 13 and are removed on their own with a single click.
- Draw cards from the stock to the waste pile; the top waste card is always available.
- Stock and waste cards may pair with each other or with available pyramid cards.
- Win by removing all 28 cards from the pyramid.
