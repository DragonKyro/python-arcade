MASTERMIND

Crack the secret 4-color code in 10 attempts or fewer.

HOW TO PLAY
- Click a color from the palette at the bottom to select it.
- Click a slot in the current row to place your selected color.
- Fill all 4 slots, then click Submit to make your guess.

FEEDBACK PEGS (shown to the right of each guess)
- Black peg: correct color in the correct position.
- White peg: correct color but in the wrong position.
- Empty: that color is not in the code.

Use the feedback to narrow down the secret code. Good luck!
