CHECKERS

Capture all of the computer's pieces or block them from moving.

HOW TO PLAY
- You are Red (bottom), the computer is Black (top).
- Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted square to move.
- Regular pieces move diagonally forward one square.
- Capture by jumping diagonally over an opponent's piece into an
  empty square beyond it.
- Multi-jumps are allowed if another capture is available after landing.

KINGS
- When a piece reaches the far row, it becomes a King.
- Kings can move and capture diagonally in both directions.

MANDATORY CAPTURES
- If a capture is available, you must take it.

WINNING
- Capture all opponent pieces, or leave them with no valid moves.
