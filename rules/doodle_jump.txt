DOODLE JUMP

Jump endlessly upward on platforms to reach the highest score.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow keys (or A/D) to move horizontally.
- The character auto-jumps when landing on a platform.
- Character wraps around screen edges (go off left, appear right).

PLATFORMS
- Green: normal, always solid.
- Blue: moves horizontally.
- Brown: breaks on contact (one use only).
- White: disappears shortly after landing.

HAZARDS
- Falling off the bottom of the screen ends the game.
- Avoid monsters (if present).

Your score is your maximum height reached. Beat your high score!
