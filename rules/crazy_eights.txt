CRAZY EIGHTS

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

HOW TO PLAY

Players: 2-4.

Each player is dealt 5 cards (7 if only 2 players). One card is turned face-up to start the discard pile. Remaining cards form the stock.

On your turn, play a card that matches the top card's suit or rank.

8s are wild: play an 8 on anything and choose the next suit.

If you cannot play, draw from the stock until you can play or the stock is empty.

First player to empty their hand wins.
