PUZZLE BUBBLE VS

Compete head-to-head against the computer in split-screen Puzzle Bubble.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow: aim the shooter.
- Space: fire a bubble.

VS MECHANICS
- Pop bubbles to send junk bubbles to your opponent.
- 3-match = 1 junk, 4-match = 2 junk, 5+ match = 3 junk.
- Dropping floating bubbles sends extra junk.
- Junk bubbles appear as gray bubbles on top of your opponent's board.

DIFFICULTY
- Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard before the match.
- Harder AI aims more accurately and shoots faster.

WINNING
- Your opponent's bubbles reach the danger line = you win.
- Your bubbles reach the danger line = you lose.
