PAC-MAN

Eat all dots while avoiding four colored ghosts.

CONTROLS
- Arrow keys to change direction.
- Pac-Man moves continuously in the chosen direction.

GHOSTS (each has unique behavior)
- Blinky (red): directly chases Pac-Man aggressively.
- Pinky (pink): ambushes by targeting 4 tiles ahead of Pac-Man.
- Inky (cyan): unpredictable, uses Blinky's position to calculate target.
- Clyde (orange): chases when far away, retreats when close.

GAMEPLAY
- Eat all dots to clear the level.
- Power pellets (large dots) turn ghosts blue and vulnerable.
- Eat blue ghosts for bonus points (200, 400, 800, 1600).
- Ghosts respawn at the center pen after being eaten.
- Fruit bonuses appear periodically in the center.

You start with 3 lives. Losing all lives ends the game.
