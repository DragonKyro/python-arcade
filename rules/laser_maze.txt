LASER MAZE
Rotate and place mirrors to guide a laser beam to one or more targets.

HOW TO PLAY
- A grid contains a laser emitter, targets, and mirror pieces.
- Rotate or place mirrors to redirect the laser beam.
- The beam must hit all targets to solve the puzzle.

RULES
1. The laser travels in a straight line until it hits a mirror, wall, or target.
2. Mirrors reflect the beam at 90 degrees according to their orientation.
3. Some mirrors are fixed; only free mirrors can be rotated or moved.
4. The beam must reach every target on the board.
5. The beam cannot pass through solid blockers.
