TETRIS

Stack falling blocks to clear lines and survive as long as possible.

CONTROLS
- Left/Right arrow: move piece sideways.
- Down arrow: soft drop (faster fall).
- Up arrow: rotate piece clockwise.
- Space: hard drop (instant drop to bottom).

SCORING
- Clear 1 line: 100 points.
- Clear 2 lines: 300 points.
- Clear 3 lines: 500 points.
- Clear 4 lines (Tetris!): 800 points.

LEVELS
- Every 10 lines cleared, the level increases and pieces fall faster.

GAME OVER
- The game ends when new pieces can no longer fit on the board.
