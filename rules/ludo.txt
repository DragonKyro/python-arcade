LUDO

A classic board game of strategy and luck for 2-4 players.

HOW TO PLAY
- Each player has 4 pieces that start in their home base.
- Roll the die on your turn. Roll a 6 to move a piece from home onto the board.
- Click a piece to move it forward by the number rolled.
- Move all 4 of your pieces to the finish area to win.

RULES
- You must roll a 6 to enter a new piece onto the starting square.
- Rolling a 6 grants an extra turn.
- Landing on an opponent's piece sends it back to their home base.
- Pieces travel around the shared outer track, then up their own colored home column.
- You must roll the exact number needed to enter the finish area.
- The first player to get all 4 pieces to the finish wins.
