CONNECT FOUR

Drop discs to connect four in a row before the computer.

HOW TO PLAY
- You are Red, the computer is Yellow.
- Click a column to drop your disc into the lowest available slot.
- Hover over columns to preview your drop.

WINNING
- Connect 4 of your discs in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line.
- If the board fills up with no winner, it's a draw.
